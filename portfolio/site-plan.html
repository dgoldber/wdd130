Projects

The only project I have publically available at the moment is the Zion Choir website. I am a Zion Choir alumnus from when I was in highschool, and run the website for a family member who is one of the current directors. I was asked to take over the site due to the site being taken down by malicious hackers, and because the previous webmaster did not have time to take care of it.

In restoring the site, I also redid the design to give it a more modern look. The website is powered by Wordpress, as it gives user-friendly tools to the directors so that they can make changes by themselves without needing to go through me, and because Wordpress allowed me to use prebuilt themes, which was important while trying to restore a dead website without backups in time for upcoming auditions.

Links to other projects may be posted here as they are available. (Code photo by Markus Spiske on Unsplash )
