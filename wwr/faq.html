Frequently Asked Questions

Why raft instead of drive?

  1. Traffic: Rafting doesn't deal with congestion or unexpected jams, you can plan your commute to take around the same amount of time each day
  2. Economy: Gas prices are going up, which makes rafting more cost-effective
  3. Ecology: Rafting is an environmentally sustainable form of travel

Wont my laptop/phone/etc. get wet?

Each raft is equipped with waterproof storage compartments so that you won't lose or soak your belongings.

When do rafts leave?

In order to fit a broad range of schedules, we have rafts leaving ever 20 minutes from 5-9 AM Monday through Saturday.

How long do trips take?

Each trip downriver typically takes 25-30 minutes.
